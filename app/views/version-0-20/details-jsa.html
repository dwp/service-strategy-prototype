Applying for New Style Job Seeker's Allowance

Eligibility

You might be able to get New Style JSA if you:

  • are 18 or over (there are some exceptions if you’re 16 or 17 - contact Jobcentre Plus for advice)
  • are under State Pension age
  • are not in full-time education
  • are available for work
  • are not working at the moment, or working less than 16 hours per week on average
  • do not have an illness or disability which stops you from working
  • live in England, Scotland or Wales
  • have worked as an an employee and paid Class 1 National Insurance contributions, usually in the last 2 to 3 years – National Insurance credits can also count

To apply online, you'll need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank or building society account details (or those of a family member or trusted friend) employment details for the past 6 months, including employer contact details and dates you worked with them
  • private pension or annuity statement letter if you get, or could get, payments from one

It usually takes 20 minutes to complete the form. You will not be able to save your answers and come back to the form later. But you'll get a chance to check and update your answers before you send it.

We will treat your personal information carefully. We may use it for any of our purposes. To learn about your information rights and how we use information, see our personal information charter.
