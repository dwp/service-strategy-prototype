Understand benefits and financial support you could get while you're looking for work

Regular payments you could get:

  • New Style Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA) if you’re working less than 16 hours a week, are under State Pension age, and have made enough National Insurance contributions over the last 2 to 3 years - find out more about New Style JSA
  • Universal Credit if you have less than £16,000 in savings and you or your partner is under State Pension age - find out more about Universal Credit
  • Pension Credit if you and your partner have both reached State Pension age - find out more about Pension Credit

You might also be able to get help with specific living costs.

If you're unsure which regular payments you could get

You can answer some questions to find out whether New Style JSA, Universal Credit or Pension Credit is best for your situation.

Help for other situations

Other help might also be available if:

    • you're on a low income
    • you're temporarily unable to work
    • you're disabled or have a health condition
    • you're caring for someone
    • you're looking after a family
    • someone has died
