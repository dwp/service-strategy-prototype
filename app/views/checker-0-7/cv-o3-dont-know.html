Being unable to work

Tell your employer that you have symptoms of coronavirus (COVID-19)

Your employer should tell you if you’re covered by their sick leave policy.

After the first 7 days of absence, they may ask you for an isolation note to show you cannot work because of coronavirus (COVID-19). You can get an isolation note from NHS 111 online.

Statutory Sick Pay (SSP)

If you cannot get sick pay from your employer, you might be able to get SSP from day one of your illness.

The law is currently changing so that if you’ve been ill from coronavirus (COVID-19) since 13 March 2020 and you’re eligible for SSP, you will get a backdated payment.

If you’re not eligible for sick pay or SSP

If you cannot get sick pay or SSP, you can check what other financial support to apply for.
