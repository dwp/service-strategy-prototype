Benefits you already get

You'll continue to get your benefit payments as normal

Your payments will not be affected if you get:

  • Jobseeker’s Allowance
  • New Style Jobseeker’s Allowance
  • Employment and Support Allowance
  • Disability Living Allowance
  • Personal Independence Payment

You do not need to attend any Jobcentre Plus appointments or medical assessments during the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak, even if you do not have symptoms of coronavirus (COVID-19).

You should not attend the jobcentre unless asked to do so for an exceptional purpose, for example to collect your Payment Exception Service vouchers.

You must still tell us about changes to your circumstances.
