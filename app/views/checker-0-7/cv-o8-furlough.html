Being made unemployed

Your employer might be able to pay some of your wages

Your employer might be able to keep you on the payroll if they're unable to operate and put you on temporary leave instead. This is known as being ‘on furlough’.

Your employer could get a grant using the Coronavirus Job Retention Scheme to help them pay for some of your wages. You can check if your employer can use it.

If your salary is reduced

If you're paid less because of these changes, you can check if you're eligible for Universal Credit.
