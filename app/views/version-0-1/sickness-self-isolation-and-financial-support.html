Check what support you could apply for

You might be able to get financial support ('benefits') if you cannot work because you're:

  • suffering from an illness or health condition
  • staying at home ('self-isolating') because you or someone you live with has symptoms of coronavirus (COVID-19)
  • at high risk from coronavirus (COVID-19) and have been told by the NHS to take extra steps to protect yourself (you're 'shielding')

Financial support you might get

Sick leave

Tell your employer about your illness or health condition. They should tell you if you’re covered by their sick leave policy.

Statutory Sick Pay (SSP)

If you're not covered by your employer's sick leave policy, you could get SSP. You must:

  • be classed as an emloyee and have done some work for your employer
  • earn an average of at least £120 a week
  • have been ill, self-isolating or shielding for at least 4 days in a row

Check how to get SSP.

New Style Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

New Style ESA gives you money to help with living costs if you're unable to work because of a health condition or disability. To get it, you must:

  • have worked as an employee or been self-employed
  • paid enough National Insurance contributions, usually in the last 2 to 3 years – National Insurance credits also count
  • not be getting the severe disability premium, or be entitled to it
  • not have got or been entitled to the severe disability premium in the last month
  • not be getting SSP from an employer

New Style ESA and coronavirus (COVID-19)

You might also be able to get New Style ESA if:

  • you or your child might have coronavirus or you’re recovering from it
  • you or your child are self-isolating
  • you're shielding

Check how to apply for New Style ESA.

Other support if you cannot work

You might be able to get other financial support if you're sick, self-isolating or shielding. This includes:

  • Universal Credit – you could get this with or instead of New Style ESA to help with living costs
  • support if you have a disability
