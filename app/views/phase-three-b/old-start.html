Check what regular payments you could get if you're looking for work

If you're looking for work, you can use this service to check whether New Style Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA), Universal Credit or Pension Credit is best for your situation.

If you have an illness, health condition or disability that limits your ability to work, you might be able to get further financial support.

You can use this service if you live in England, Scotland or Wales.

It takes most people around 5 minutes.

Start now

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Further help might be available if:

  • you're on a low income
  • you're temporarily unable to work
  • you're disabled or have a health condition
  • you're caring for someone
  • you're looking after a family
  • someone has died
