Coronavirus (COVID-19): help and advice if someone dies

When someone dies it can affect people in different ways. There’s often lots to do which can feel overwhelming.

But there is a range of support available to help you if you need it. This includes:

  • a step by step guide on what to do when someone dies
  • information and support from your local council’s bereavement service
  • NHS advice on dealing with bereavement, grief and loss
  • support, advice and information from Cruse Bereavement Care, including coping with grief during the coronavius (COVID-19) outbreak
  • guidance on what to do after a death from Citizens Advice
