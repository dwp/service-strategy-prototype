Overview

You could get Pension Credit if you and your partner have both reached State Pension age and your weekly income is below £173.75 (for single people) or £265.20 (for couples). You might still be able to get it even if you have savings, have a pension or own your home

You’ll also need to take reasonable steps to look for work. You must still follow the guidance on working safely during coronavirus.

Other benefits that you might be able to get

If you don’t think this is the right benefit for you, or you want to see what additional benefits you might be able to get, see:

  • Support if you're on a low income
  • Support if you're temporarily unable to work
  • Support if you're disabled or have a health condition
  • Support if you're caring for someone
  • Support if you're looking after a family
  • Support if someone has died
  • Help with specific living costs
